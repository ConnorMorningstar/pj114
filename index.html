The biggest and brightest Supermoon of 2025!

This Full Moon was not only visually stunning due to its supermoon status, but also symbolically important. Ancient cultures named it the Beaver Moon for seasonal activities aligned with nature. Astrologically, its brightness in Taurus symbolized heightened emotions and a time for grounding and introspection. It's a powerful moment in both celestial observation and personal reflection.

Date: 5, Nov, 2025

The Moon will be close to the Pleiades star cluster

The Moon passed very close to the Pleiades star cluster in Taurus. This created a beautiful sky view, with the nearly full Moon shining close to the cluster of bright, blue “Seven Sisters” stars. It was a highlight for stargazers.

Date: 6, Nov, 2025

The third confirmed interstellar object to visit our solar system

The third interstellar object which is also called comet 3I/ATLAS is an interstellar comet—meaning it came from outside our solar system—discovered in July 2025 by astronomers using the ATLAS telescope. It's one of only three such known visitors to pass through the solar system.

Date: 1, July, 2025

The U.S. government shut down

The U.S. government shut down because Congress failed to pass the budget for the new fiscal year. This led to the furlough of about 900,000 federal workers, and millions more worked without pay. Essential services continued, but many agencies closed or slowed down
